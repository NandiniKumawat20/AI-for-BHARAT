Team Name: Team Techno

## Problem Statement
AI for Communities, Access & Public Impact – Healthcare

Millions of people in rural, low-income, and underserved communities lack access to timely and reliable healthcare information. Due to shortage of doctors, long travel distances, language barriers, and low health awareness, people often depend on self-medication, local myths, or delayed treatment. This leads to worsening health conditions and avoidable emergencies.

## Existing System & Limitations
- Limited access to doctors in rural areas
- Overcrowded government hospitals
- No clear guidance on symptom seriousness
- Unsafe self-medication practices
- No family-based healthcare management tools
- Language and literacy barriers

## Objectives
- Help users understand and assess symptoms
- Guide users with safe first-aid instructions
- Decide when hospital care is urgently required
- Support healthcare management for multiple family members
- Reduce unsafe self-medication and misinformation

## Functional Requirements
- Single user account with multiple family profiles
- Symptom input using text or voice
- AI-based symptom analysis
- Risk classification: Low / Medium / High
- Safe first-aid guidance (no medicine prescription)
- Emergency alert for serious conditions
- Government (civil) hospital locator with OPD details
- Multilingual support

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Works in low-bandwidth environments
- Fast and reliable response
- Secure handling of user data
- Simple and accessible user interface

## Constraints
- Not a doctor replacement
- No diagnosis or prescription
- Only decision-support and guidance
